BuckIt

Do stuff. Make friends. BuckIt is a platform where people can keep track of all the activities they ever wanted to do creating their own personal bucket list. Cannot think of activities of your own or looking for others to join you in completeing the activity> BuckIt allows you to search for activities created by others and allows you to join.
